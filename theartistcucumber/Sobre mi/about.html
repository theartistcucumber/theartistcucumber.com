Alejandro Rodríguez Carchenilla, or better known as The Artist Cucumber, is an illustrator and character designer.

He works every day from his iPad at Procreate, as he is someone who likes to work outside his home. But you have to be honest, also master Photoshop perfectly and have no choice but to work from home.

He studied Visual Design of Digital Content at U-Tad University (Madrid) and made his first steps in the Ilion animation studio. From cafes, parks or beaches, draw all kinds of character and creatures for businesses and particulares. Between adventures, he works on his own projects on the visiual representation of fears, phobias, etc.
